Remine   India

Welcome to Remine India, the leading company in recycling e-waste and Li batteries. We provide innovative solutions for businesses and individuals to dispose of their electronic waste responsibly, while also protecting the environment.

Our mission is to reduce the harmful impact of electronic waste on the environment by recycling and repurposing the valuable materials found in electronic devices. We are committed to helping our customers meet their sustainability goals by providing a reliable and convenient recycling service.

Why Choose Remine India?

    "Remine India: E-Waste and Li Battery Recycling"

  • Environmentally friendly: We prioritize the protection of the environment and strive to minimize the amount of e-waste that ends up in landfills.
  • Convenient: Our recycling process is hassle-free, and we offer pick-up services for businesses and individuals.
  • Secure: We follow strict security protocols to ensure that any sensitive data is properly disposed of and destroyed.
  • Transparent: We provide full transparency in our recycling process and give our customers the peace of mind that their electronic waste is being disposed of responsibly.
